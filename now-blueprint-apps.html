Overhauling Blueprint Apps

A collaboration with Engineering to build powerful new workflows, empowering administrators to support their teams more efficiently. And making it look good on mobile, and in German, to boot.

The Product

Jamf Now is a cloud-based Apple device management platform for small business. More than 20 million devices are managed on Jamf Now.

A significant number of our customers manage devices as a secondary or tertiary responsibility, so our job is to ensure that anyone, regardless of technical proficiency, feels confident.

Our UX team uses five personas spanning all the levels of proficiency and responsibility we've come to know from our users.

The Challenges

  • When adding an app, users are given overlapping interfaces with competing similarities.
  • Modals on Jamf Now are not designed with mobile in mind. Content is pushed out of frame quickly, even on the largest portable screens
  • We were dealing with a cramped interface. We needed to create breathing room for extensibility.
  • The solution needed to provide power and flexibility for beginner and advanced users, Standard tier and Plus tier users. One-size-fits-all.
  • We needed to come up with something fast. The Product Owner and I agreed that this could ship ahead of those upcoming roadmap features, but we had a short window before Engineering was unavailable.
