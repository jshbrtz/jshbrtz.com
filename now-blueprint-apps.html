Overhauling Blueprint Apps

A collaboration with Engineering to build powerful new workflows, empowering administrators to support their teams more efficiently. And making it look good on mobile, and in German, to boot.

The Product

Jamf Now is a cloud-based Apple device management platform for small business. More than 20 million devices are managed on Jamf Now.

A significant number of our customers manage devices as a secondary or tertiary responsibility, so our job is to ensure that anyone, regardless of technical proficiency, feels confident.

Our UX team uses five personas spanning all the levels of proficiency and responsibility we've come to know from our users.

The Problem

We needed to integrate new functionality into a workflow that administrators use to assign apps to devices. The thing is, this workflow was already stretched to its limits.

Literally. We ran out of space on desktop, and the mobile experience was so overcrowded that it was unusable. We had to come up with a new, better experience, and fast. Our engineers had a two-week window to fit this in before they had to move on.

Without time for an external usability study, we used lean methods to gather internal feedback from our customer-facing teams.
