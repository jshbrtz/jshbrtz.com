The Product

Jamf Now is a cloud-based Apple device management platform for small business. More than 20 million devices are managed on Jamf Now.

A significant number of our customers manage devices as a secondary or tertiary responsibility, so our job is to ensure that anyone, regardless of technical proficiency, feels confident.

Our UX team uses five personas spanning all the levels of proficiency and responsibility we've come to know from our users.
